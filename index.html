STARS ARTS CENTER



Stars Arts Center is an art center located in Rubavu, Rwanda. The Center, founded in 2001 ,it is located along Kivu Lake. It features fine art exhibitions, art classes and studios,a library with over 100 titles, and the ART GARDEN...Stars Arts Center is an active and awesome community center with a specific remit to encourage arts practice and to provide facilities such as theatre space, gallery space, venues for musical performance, workshop areas, educational facilities(as books).
