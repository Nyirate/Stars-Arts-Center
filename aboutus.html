Main activities

  • Art Garden

    • Art Garden is an area designed by our Art Center with a goal to bring "art, artists, and the community together through different activities in an open-air setting," the area serves as a gallery without walls and a space for creating art outside. We invite people and certain artists,and through this people get the oppotunity to meet their favorite artists and other inspiring ones..This happens the last Friday of every month.learn more


  • Exhibitions

    • Art exhibition is traditionally the space in which art objects meet an audience,this is also called exposition.Such expositions may present pictures, drawings, video, sound, installation, performance, interactive art, new media art or sculptures by individual artists, groups of artists.The art works may be presented in museums, art halls, art clubs or private art galleries.At Stars Arts Center exhibition is done thrice a year.learn more


  • Education

    • Art education includes performing arts like dance, music, theater, and visual arts like drawing, painting, sculpture, and design works.Design works include design in jewelry, pottery, weaving and fabrics.Stars Arts Center provides education to different people;children,arts students,adults,etc..The Arts Center provides 3 courses per week to the public and has students who attend classes everyday except in the Weekend,children's program is only available in the Weekend.learn more
